About Us

Men Without Yachts Club allows a place for men without yachts to come together and connect! Being a part of the club has many benefits!

  • Meet other men without yachts
  • Explore alternative leisure
  • Group therapy
  • And more!

We meet every Wednesday at 4:00 pm at Zoop University Library!
